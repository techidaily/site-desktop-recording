## Digital Video Capturing Demystified: Systematic Guide

Screen recorders have become an important part of our lives since the internet boom. Today gamers use it for recording their gaming sessions. And any normal person may also want to record their favorite video, or music to save to their device. Screencastify is a leading screen recorder application that is available for Google chrome.

## 1\. Key Features

Some of the key features of the Screencastify application are:

* Equipped with built-in tools for Google chrome that can be installed without any storage need.
* Allows secured video recording and sharing tools for all your needs.
* Effective screen recording at the different video and audio quality with instant sharing feature.
* Simple yet efficient user interface that can turn any video into classic content.
* Annotation availability to grab the attention of the viewers in the editing section.
* Engagement monitoring for Realtime feedback and response from viewers after sharing.
* Adding voice overs to the videos made simple with quick editing settings.
* Video outputs are made available in different formats to work on different platforms.
