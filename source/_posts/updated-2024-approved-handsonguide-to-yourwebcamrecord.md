## Part 2\. Key Features

Here are the key features of YouCam webcam recorder that make it stand out.

Record Webcam Videos – The primary function of YouCam is to record webcam videos. On top of that, it can record live video calls on different apps such as Skype, Hangouts. In fact, you can also record video conference on U Meeting and U Webinar. Moreover, you can record live streams on YouTube and Facebook.

Screen Capture – YouCam can not only record webcam but also the entire screen. This is useful when you are attending video calls and video conferences. Besides, you can capture screenshot of yourself that is being shown on the webcam using YouCam screen recorder.

Enhance Webcam Video – Unlike other webcam recorders, YouCam lets you enhance the video output by providing you with visual filters and graphical effects. You can apply them to webcam video, live streaming video, as well as static images. There are over 200 effects available to choose from.

Add Face Tags – YouCam lets you add face tags to your video so that you can search for any particular photo or video instantly. This is useful for video creators who are regular in video creation, and they can categorize the videos and images with face tags for easy searching.

Settings Customization – You can customize the lighting as well as smoothen the skin tone as per your preference. Besides, you can add overlays as available and required. You can also make drawings live as well as highlight mouse cursor movements while using PiP mode.

Intuitive User Interface – The user interface is clean and intuitive. There are few options available so that you can find the option of your requirement instantly. You can operate the software without prior knowledge. There is also a library available where you can find your recordings, and you can search for any recording with face tags and even datewise.
